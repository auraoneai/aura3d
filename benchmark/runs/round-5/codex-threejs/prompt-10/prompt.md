# Prompt 10: Product Viewer With Provided Sneaker

Build a product viewer using the provided `sneaker.glb`: centered, auto-scaled, on a plinth, with studio lights, orbit controls, and a rotating turntable.

## Assets

Use only:

```text
benchmark/assets/sneaker.glb
```

No asset discovery, remote search, substitute model, or invented asset path is allowed.

## Required Visual Evidence

- The sneaker model must be loaded and visible.
- The sneaker must be centered and auto-scaled.
- A plinth or product base must be visible.
- Studio lighting must make the material readable.
- Orbit controls and turntable rotation must be implemented.
- Invented asset paths count as failures for the asset-path metric.
