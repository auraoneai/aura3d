## What was built

A Three.js bar-chart visualization in `source/`, all procedural (no assets):

- **36 bars** — a 6×6 grid of individual box meshes on a centered ground grid.
- **Animated heights** — each bar eases toward an independent random target; grows from 0 on load, re-randomizes every 4 s and on the **Randomize heights** button.
- **Color by height** — a blue→green→yellow→red HSL heatmap recomputed each frame, so short bars are blue and tall bars are red.
- **Hover-highlight** — a raycaster detects the bar under the pointer, brightens it with an emissive glow, and shows a cursor tooltip with grid coordinate (`C3 · R5`) and live value; previous bar is restored on move-off.
- **Orbit camera** — `OrbitControls` (drag/zoom/pan, clamped above ground).
- **Readable axis labels** — `CSS2DRenderer` DOM overlays: column ticks `C1–C6`, row ticks `R1–R6`, a vertical value axis with numeric ticks `0.0–6.0`, and axis titles **Column (X) / Row (Z) / Value (Y)**.
