Notes:
- The app renders a 6x6 grid, 36 bars total.
- Heights animate toward changing random targets.
- Bar color is derived from height.
- Axis labels for X Axis, Z Axis, and Height are visible in the 3D scene.
- Hover-highlight is implemented with Three.js raycasting: hover a bar to turn it white, slightly enlarge it, and update the readout.
- No assets were used. `llms.txt` was not present in the provided bundle, so I used only the local scaffold/context files available.
