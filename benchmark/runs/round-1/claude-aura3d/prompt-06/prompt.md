# Prompt 06: Mini-Golf Hole

Build a mini-golf hole: a flat green with one obstacle, a ball with physics, click-to-aim-and-shoot, score counter, follow camera on the ball.

## Assets

None. This prompt must be built procedurally.

## Required Visual Evidence

- A mini-golf green must be visible.
- One obstacle must be visible.
- A ball must be visible.
- Aim or shot interaction must be present.
- Score counter must be visible.
- Camera framing must follow or emphasize the ball.
