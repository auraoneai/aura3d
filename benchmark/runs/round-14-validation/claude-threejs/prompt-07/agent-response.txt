- **Studio lighting**: hemisphere fill + key/fill/rim directional lights + overhead point "softbox", with `PCFSoftShadowMap` soft shadows on a ground plane.
- **Environment map**: `RoomEnvironment` baked through `PMREMGenerator` → `scene.environment` for IBL + reflections (procedural, no external assets).
- **Orbit controls**: `OrbitControls` with damping, distance/polar limits.
- ACES Filmic tone mapping + sRGB output for correct PBR appearance.
