# Prompt 07: Material Lab

Build a material lab: 5 spheres with metal, glass, rubber, emissive, and clearcoat materials under controlled studio lighting with an environment map and orbit controls.

## Assets

None. This prompt must be built procedurally.

## Required Visual Evidence

- Five spheres must be visible.
- The five material types must look visually distinct.
- Studio lighting must be visible through highlights, reflections, or soft shadows.
- Environment lighting or reflection must be present.
- Orbit controls must be enabled.
