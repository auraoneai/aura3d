## Summary

Built a **Neon Tunnel Flythrough** in `source/src/main.ts` using `three@0.165.0` and bundled official addons only.

**What it does (maps to required visual evidence):**
- **Tunnel/tube interior** — `THREE.TubeGeometry` swept along a procedural winding `CatmullRomCurve3` (6 turns), rendered `BackSide` so the camera flies *inside* it.
- **Emissive segments** — a procedural canvas neon-grid `emissiveMap` on the wall, plus 120 bright `TorusGeometry` ring segments along the path cycling/pulsing through a 5-color neon palette.
- **Bloom/glow** — `EffectComposer` → `RenderPass` → `UnrealBloomPass` (strength 1.25, radius 0.8, threshold 0.12), with ACES tone mapping.

- **Camera animation** — camera is driven along the curve via `getPointAt(t)` each frame, looking further down the tunnel with a subtle roll; the scrolling emissive grid adds a sense of speed. Any captured frame reads as a forward flythrough.
