## Summary

Built a complete mini-golf hole in raw Three.js 0.165.0 (Vite + strict TypeScript) in `source/src/main.ts`.

**What's implemented:**
- **Green** — flat striped `PlaneGeometry` surface with shadow receiving.
- **One obstacle** — a red brick block mid-course (AABB collision).
- **Ball with physics** — white sphere with velocity integration, friction (exponential decay), rolling rotation, wall/obstacle bounce (restitution), sub-stepped for stability, and hole-capture detection.
- **Click-to-aim-and-shoot** — slingshot model: pointer-down + drag from the ball projects onto the ground plane via raycaster; a yellow `ArrowHelper` shows direction and grows with power; release shoots and increments the stroke count.
- **Score counter** — fixed HUD overlay showing strokes, live power %, rolling/ready status, and a "Holed in N strokes" win message.
- **Follow camera** — perspective camera that smoothly lerps to track the ball's position and look target.
