The procedural solar system app is built and the production build passes (typecheck + Vite build clean).

**What it does** (`source/src/main.ts`, raw Three.js 0.165.0):
- **Sun + 6 planets** — a bright `MeshBasicMaterial` sun with a `PointLight`, and Mercury → Saturn each on its own rotating pivot.
- **Visibly different orbits/speeds** — distances 18/27/37/48/62/78 with faster inner orbits (0.90 → 0.18 rad/s), each drawn with a visible orbit ring; Saturn has a ring.
- **Readable labels** — `CSS2DRenderer` HTML chips floating above the Sun and every planet.
- **Bloom on the sun** — `EffectComposer` + `RenderPass` + `UnrealBloomPass` (threshold 0.6 so only the bright sun glows).
- **Orbit camera** — `OrbitControls` with damping, positioned at (0, 70, 150) framing the whole system; plus a starfield backdrop.
