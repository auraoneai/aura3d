## Summary

Built a procedural **Material Lab** in raw Three.js (0.165.0) at `source/src/main.ts`, using only bundled addons (`OrbitControls`, `RoomEnvironment`).

**How each visual requirement is met:**
- **Five spheres** — one row of 5 sphere meshes, evenly spaced.
- **Five distinct materials** — Metal (`MeshStandardMaterial`, metalness 1, low roughness), Glass (`MeshPhysicalMaterial`, transmission 1, IOR 1.5), Rubber (matte high-roughness standard), Emissive (green `emissiveIntensity` 2.5), Clearcoat (`MeshPhysicalMaterial`, clearcoat 1 over blue base).
- **Studio lighting** — key/fill/rim `DirectionalLight`s plus two softbox panels with `PointLight`s for crisp highlights; PCF soft shadows cast onto a floor.
- **Environment lighting/reflection** — `RoomEnvironment` baked through `PMREMGenerator` into `scene.environment`, reflected in the metal/glass/clearcoat spheres.
- **Orbit controls** — `OrbitControls` with damping enabled.

ACES tone mapping + sRGB output for correct studio look.
