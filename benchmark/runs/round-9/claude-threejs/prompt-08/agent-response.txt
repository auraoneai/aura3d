## Summary

Built a procedural city block in `source/src/main.ts` using raw Three.js (`three` + bundled `three/addons/controls/OrbitControls.js`).

**What it renders:**
- **20 buildings** — a 5×4 grid of `BoxGeometry` towers with deterministic (seeded) **varying heights** (16–60 units).
- **Windows** — each facade gets a canvas-generated colour map + matching emissive map, so windows are visible by day and glow at night.
- **Streets** — an asphalt road grid laid between the lots with dashed centre-line markings on a dark ground plane.
- **Street lights** — poles with lamp heads and `PointLight`s along the avenues (lit at night).
- **Day/night toggle** — a top-left button that swaps the sky (gradient day sky ↔ dark starfield), fog, hemisphere/ambient/sun (→ moon) lighting, window emissives, and street-light glow. Starts in daytime.
- OrbitControls (drag/scroll) and shadow-mapped sun.
