Assumptions/run notes:
- Procedural only, no assets used.
- The scene renders a 6x6 grid, exactly 36 bars.
- Bar heights animate between randomized values.
- Bar color maps to height from blue/low through warm red/high.
- Hover highlight is implemented with Three.js raycasting: moving the pointer over a bar enlarges it slightly, adds emissive highlight, and updates the bottom-left readout with column, row, and value.
- Orbit camera is enabled with `OrbitControls`.
- Axis labels and tick labels are rendered with `CSS2DRenderer`.
