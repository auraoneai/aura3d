## Summary

Built a sneaker product viewer in `source/src/main.ts` using `three` 0.165.0 and bundled official addons (`GLTFLoader`, `OrbitControls`, `RoomEnvironment`).

What it does, mapped to the required visual evidence:
- **Model loaded & visible** — loads `sneaker.glb` via `GLTFLoader`.
- **Centered & auto-scaled** — computes the bounding box, scales the largest dimension to a fixed target size, recenters horizontally, and rests the model's bottom on the plinth top.
- **Plinth/base visible** — a light cylinder plinth (with a dark shadow-catching floor beneath).
- **Studio lighting readable** — `RoomEnvironment` IBL via `PMREMGenerator` plus ambient + key/fill/rim directional lights, ACES tone mapping, sRGB output, and soft shadows.
- **Orbit controls + turntable** — `OrbitControls` with damping aimed at the model center, and a parent pivot group that auto-rotates around Y each frame.

The sneaker is referenced at `/benchmark/assets/sneaker.glb`, which is the scaffold-provided copy under `source/public/benchmark/assets/sneaker.glb` (Vite serves `public/` at the site root) — no invented path.
