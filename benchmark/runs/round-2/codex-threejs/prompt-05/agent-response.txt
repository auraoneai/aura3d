Notes:
- The scene contains a procedural `6 x 6` grid, 36 total bars.
- Heights continuously animate toward random values.
- Bar color maps to height from blue/green through yellow/red.
- Axis labels and tick labels are rendered with `CSS2DRenderer`.
- Hover-highlight is implemented with raycasting: moving the pointer over a bar brightens it and shows a white selection ring at its base.
- I did not find `llms.txt` in the provided prompt directory or parent tree, so I used only the provided scaffold/context files.
