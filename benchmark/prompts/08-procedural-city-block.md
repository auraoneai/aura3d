# Prompt 08: Procedural City Block

Build a procedurally generated city block: 20 box buildings of varying heights with windows, streets, street lights, and a day/night toggle that changes lighting and sky.

## Assets

None. This prompt must be built procedurally.

## Required Visual Evidence

- About 20 buildings must be visible.
- Building heights must vary.
- Windows must be visible.
- Streets and street lights must be visible.
- Day/night toggle must be visible and must change lighting and sky.
