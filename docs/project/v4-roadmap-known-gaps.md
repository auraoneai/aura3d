# V4 Known Gaps

> Historical note: This V4 document is retained as project history after the V9 parity reset. Current planning, claim boundaries, and code-backed parity status live in `docs/project/v9-roadmap-status.md`, `docs/project/v9-roadmap-parity-matrix.md`, and `docs/project/v9-roadmap-three-js-parity-plan.md`. Treat unchecked tasks or old claims here as historical unless they are restated in the V9 docs.


These gaps remain even after Milestones 0-17 pass.

## Product Gaps

- `pnpm v4:release` does not exist yet.
- Final release readiness has not been audited.
- The public claim remains conditional until the release gate passes.

## Three.js Gaps

- Broad Three.js replacement remains blocked.
- Full Three.js API compatibility remains blocked.
- Three.js ecosystem breadth, example count, community support, and plugin coverage are not matched.

## Rendering And Asset Gaps

- Full glTF ecosystem parity remains blocked.
- Full PBR/material extension parity remains blocked.
- Full compressed texture ecosystem parity remains blocked.
- Full WebGPU parity remains blocked.
- Broad performance superiority remains blocked.

## Engine Scope Gaps

- Unity replacement remains blocked.
- Unreal replacement remains blocked.
- Full game engine replacement remains blocked.
- Full commercial DCC pipeline parity remains blocked.

## Evidence Boundary

Screenshots, visual scores, and package smokes are necessary evidence, but they do not by themselves unblock broad replacement claims.
