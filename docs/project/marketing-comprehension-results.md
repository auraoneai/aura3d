# Marketing Comprehension Results

## Status

Not run yet. This is an external/manual proof item.

The automated marketing checks currently verify wording, links, copy buttons,
and absence of internal release-cycle or draft-status language. They do not
prove that people understand the product.

## Test Method

Show the marketing site to three people who do not know the Aura3D codebase:

- An indie React developer.
- A 3D artist who has used Three.js.
- A non-technical product manager.

Ask each person:

1. In one sentence, what does this product do?
2. Who is it for?
3. What would you install first?
4. What would you try building first?
5. What part of the page was unclear?

## Pass Criteria

The page passes only if:

- At least two of three people identify Aura3D as a browser 3D authoring layer
  for developers and AI coding agents.
- At least two of three people understand that users bring their own assets.
- At least two of three people identify `create-aura3d` or the starter
  templates as the first install path.
- No participant describes the product as an internal release, research demo,
  or hidden backend service.
