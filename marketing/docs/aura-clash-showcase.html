Aura Clash

Aura Clash is the Aura3D game showcase built to demonstrate a polished browser fighting game workflow with typed GLB assets, Quaternius CC0 characters, modular city arenas, responsive UI, accessibility controls, evidence routes, screenshot capture, and deployment-evidence gates.

What it proves

Typed assets

Fighter, arena, and playable-scene GLBs are registered through Aura3D typed asset references.

Game loop

Movement, guard, dash, attacks, meter, AI pressure, timer, and round result states are implemented.

Developer evidence

The evidence route exposes provenance, typed asset names, controls, route surface, and open QA gates.

Accessibility

Reduced motion, reduced flash, and high contrast controls are part of the showcase UI.

Deployment gates

The deploy-check route tracks static metadata, route coverage, screenshot proof, and live deployment evidence still required.
